### UNIT 7: INTERNATIONAL ENTRY STRATEGIES

#### 1. Modes of Entry into Foreign Markets (Foreign Markets me enter karne ke modes)
Global expansion ke liye firm ko aisa entry mode select karna hota hai jo control, risk, aur resource commitment ko balance kare:
1.  **Exporting (Niryat):**
    *   *Indirect Exporting:* Domestic-based trading agents ke through sale karna. Low risk, minimal capital involvement, aur zero control.
    *   *Direct Exporting:* Foreign buyers/distributors ko directly self-manage karke sale karna. Direct control aur client feedback badhta hai par cost zyada hai.
2.  **Contractual Modes (Contracts-based entries):**
    *   *Licensing:* Foreign firm ko technology, patents ya brands use karne ki permission dena in exchange for a royalty fee. Low capital requirements aur low risk, lekin isme IP transfer hone par future competitor banne ka darr rehta hai aur zero control hota hai.
    *   *Franchising:* Licensing ka specialized form jisme franchisor complete business model, brand name, aur standards provide karta hai (e.g., McDonald's, KFC) for a fee. Rapid expansion ke liye best hai par operational quality control maintain karna complex hota hai.
3.  **Investment Modes (FDI-based entries):**
    *   *International Joint Venture (IJV):* Ek foreign company aur ek local company aapas me milkar naya independent business start karti hain aur management control share karti hain. Local market knowledge aur government compliance aasan ho jata hai, aur risks split hote hain. Halanki, partner conflicts aur shared profits iske drawbacks hain.
    *   *Wholly Owned Subsidiary (WOS):* 100% ownership aur operational control foreign land me hona.
        *   *Greenfield Investment:* Scratch (bilkul shuru) se nayi facility/factory banana. Setup customized hota hai par yeh slower aur high-risk hota hai.
        *   *Acquisition (M&A):* Kisi established local firm ko kharidna (buyout). Market entrance instantaneous hota hai par systems integration aur cultural issues complex hote hain.

#### 2. Factors Influencing the Choice of Entry Mode (Entry mode choose karne ke factors)
Entry mode ka decision company ke internal resources aur host country ke external conditions par depend karta hai:

| Factor Type | Variable | Impact on Entry Mode Choice (Hinglish) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Internal Factors** | **Firm Size & Resources** | Large firms high-commitment modes (FDI/WOS) choose karti hain. Small firms low-commitment modes (Exporting/Licensing) ke sath jati hain. |
| | **International Experience** | Experience badhne se foreign risks ka confidence badhta hai, jo direct entries/FDI ko encourage karta hai. |
| | **Product Complexity / Tech** | High-tech ya critical intellectual properties ki leakages rokne ke liye high control modes (WOS) use hote hain. |
| **External Factors** | **Market Size & Growth** | Badi aur growing markets high-commitment modes (FOS/JV) justify karti hain. Small markets me exporting best rehta hai. |
| | **Government Policies & Tariffs**| High tariffs aur regulatory bans hone par exporting bekaar ho jata hai, isliye local JVs ya manufacturing plants (FDI) setup karne padte hain. |
| | **Socio-Cultural Distance** | Culture aur environment bohot different ho toh local partner ke expertise ke liye Joint Ventures select karna safe rehta hai. |

#### 3. Theories of Internationalization (Internationalization ki main theories)
*   **The Uppsala Model (Stages Model):** Is model ke mutabik internationalization ek sequential aur slow process hai jo experiential learning par chalti hai. Companies pehle un countries ko choose karti hain jo *psychically close* (cultural and legal similarities) hon, aur low-commitment modes (exporting via agents) se start karti hain. Jaise-jaise experience badhta hai, companies subsidiaries aur manufacturing (high commitment) me enter karti hain.
*   **The Network Model:** Yeh model manta hai ki international expansion relational networks par chalta hai. Global networks me relationships banana aur maintain karna hi firms ko aage badhata hai, sequential stages ki zaroorat nahi hoti.
*   **Born-Global / International Entrepreneurship Theory:** Uppsala model ke opposite. Isme startups aur tech companies (Born Global) launch hote hi cross-border sales start kar deti hain. Digital networks, niche global demands, aur entrepreneur connections iske base hain.

### UNIT 8: ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURES

#### 1. Organizational Factors & Elements
Global strategy ko execute karne ke liye companies ko in char factors ko coordinate karna padta hai:
1.  **Structure (Dhaancha):** Division of work, reporting hierarchy, aur departments integration.
2.  **Management Processes:** Planning, budgeting, evaluation cycles, aur information flows.
3.  **People:** Recruitment, training, compensation package, aur transfers (expatriate policies).
4.  **Culture:** Corporate values, employees' mutual values, and behavior patterns.

#### 2. Types of International Organizational Structures (Structures ke main types)
*   **International Division Structure:** Domestic operations alag chalti hain, aur ek independent division saare foreign operations ko handle karta hai. Expansion ke early stages me helpful hai par domestic aur international managers me disputes badhata hai.
*   **Global Functional Structure:** Functions ke basis par setup (Production, R&D, Marketing, Finance):
    *   *Characteristics:* High centralization. Key decisions Headquarter me hote hain. Country managers ke paas rights nahi hote.
    *   *Pros:* Functional specialization, operational efficiencies, economies of scale, and no resource duplication.
    *   *Cons:* Local needs ke adaptation me delay aur inefficiency.
*   **Geographic (Area) Division Structure:** Areas/Regions ke basis par setup (e.g., Europe, Asia-Pacific, North America):
    *   *Characteristics:* High decentralization. Country/Regional managers local decisions khud le sakte hain.
    *   *Pros:* Excellent local adaptation and custom products.
    *   *Cons:* Functions ka har area me duplicate hona, jisse costs badh jati hain; product standardization hard ho jata hai.
*   **Multi-Business Global Product Division Structure:** Product lines ke base par organization setup. Har division global level par apne product ke liye fully responsible hai.
    *   *Pros:* Product innovation aur global standard designs par control.
    *   *Cons:* Functional specialization divide ho jati hai aur resources duplicate hote hain.
*   **Hybrid Structure:** Alag-alag features ko mix karna (e.g., R&D centrally setup aur marketing regions me geographically distributed).
*   **Transnational Structure:** Ek complex network model (N-form) jo global scale, local response, aur organization learning teeno ko target karta hai.
    *   *Characteristics:*
        *   Assets aur competencies split hoti hain par aapas me highly dependent (reciprocal dependency) hoti hain.
        *   Koi ek dimension (geographical ya functional) dominate nahi karta.
        *   Dual reporting systems aur mutual integration forums use hote hain.
        *   Collaborative mindset aur shared values par control chalta hai.
    *   *Limitation:* Operations complex hoti hain aur administrative costs bohot high hoti hain.

### UNIT 9: STRATEGIC ALLIANCES

#### 1. Concept of Strategic Alliances (Strategic Alliances kya hai?)
Strategic alliance do ya do se zyada independent companies ke beech ek voluntary agreement hai, jisme resources, tech, aur assets share kiye jate hain taaki mutual targets achieve ho sakein, par dono companies apna separate legal identity maintain rakhti hain.

#### 2. Types of Strategic Alliances (Alliances ke types)
*   **Equity-Based Alliances (Equity sharing):**
    *   *International Joint Ventures (IJVs):* Partners milkar tisri independent entity banate hain aur aapas me equity aur control split karte hain (e.g., Sony-Ericsson).
    *   *Minority Equity Alliances:* Ek partner doosre company me minority shares buy karke long-term alliance build karta hai.
*   **Non-Equity (Contractual) Alliances (Bina equity share kiye):**
    *   Yahan agreements purely contracts par based hote hain, shares share nahi hote.
    *   *Examples:* R&D contract agreements, joint marketing/distribution campaigns, subcontracting.

#### 3. Strategic Value & Sources of Value Creation (Alliance ki value creation)
Alliances value create karte hain through:
1.  **Complementary Resources Integration:** Jab dono partners ki strengths alag hon (e.g., ek ke paas high technology hai aur doosre ke paas local distribution aur relations hain).
2.  **Risk & Cost Sharing:** Heavy R&D ya capital projects ka risk split karna.
3.  **Speed to Market:** Local setup ka use karke fast entry aur organic setup ki delay se bachna.
4.  **Learning & Skill Transfer:** Ek doosre se systems, technical styles, aur management skills seekhna.
5.  **Regulatory Navigation:** Restricted sectors (e.g., defense ya telecom) me enter karne ke liye local partner ke sath associate hona.

#### 4. Stages of Alliance Formation (Alliance banane ke stages)
Alliance life cycle char main stages me chalta hai:

```mermaid
    A[1. Partner Analysis & Strategy] --> B[2. Negotiation & Contract]
    B --> C[3. Implementation & Management]
    C --> D[4. Evaluation & Exit/Restructuring]
```

1.  **Partner Analysis & Strategic Fit:**
    *   Alliance ke goals aur targets set karna.
    *   Sahi partner evaluate aur identify karna.
    *   **Strategic Fit:** Check karna ki resource synergy, target synchronization, aur culture fit hai ya nahi.
2.  **Negotiation & Contract Design:**
    *   Alliances ka structure select karna (Equity vs. Contractual).
    *   Contracts draft karna: roles, profit splits, governance control, aur operational guidelines set karna.
    *   Disputes clearance systems aur exit strategy (termination clauses) design karna.
3.  **Implementation & Management:**
    *   Work processes setup karna, communications guidelines banana, aur joint committees construct karna.
    *   Trust aur corporate relations build karna.
    *   Cultural integration handle karna (different corporate aur country cultures ko bridge karna).
4.  **Evaluation & Exit/Restructuring:**
    *   Targets ke base par performance check karna.
    *   Alliance ko expand karna ya positive values na aane par wind-down (exit) karna.
